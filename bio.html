Thank you for coming to my page! So I'm Ingmar. A little about myself for those interested I'm a 23 year old up and coming engineer who graduated out of Hunter College in December 2020. I'm a first generation Albanian American (forza shqiperia) born and raised in New York. I started out my college journey with the idea that I would be pursuing physical therapy since the physics of the human body and how it works really interested me (and still do) but really didn't feel as engaged until I took a comp sci class and loved the material. I enjoy solving problems and being able to continually learn new technology and am very proud of myself for 'learning how to learn' and being able to help others with their studies/life/tech as well.

As far as tech experience goes, I've worked as a discrete mathematics TA in the past and honestly enjoy the mathematics side of cs a lot, and have experience with a lot of different tech stacks. Almost all my foundational CS work was with C++ and my others were done with different languages/frameworks including python and javascript and used React, Node JS, express, MongoDB, MySQL, flask. My main interests are in software engineering, AI and Machine learning, and computer architecture which is where I hope to pivot to in the long term. Let me know if you have any questions!
